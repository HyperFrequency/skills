# arXiv Research Search

Source: https://mcpmarket.com/tools/skills/arxiv-research-search

## Use When

- You need to search arXiv for academic papers relevant to trading strategies.
- Researching quantitative finance, ML for trading, or market microstructure papers.
- Building literature reviews for strategy development.

## Inputs To Ask For

- Research topic or keywords (e.g., "momentum strategies", "transformer models finance").
- Time range for papers (recent only, or historical).
- Specific categories (q-fin, cs.LG, stat.ML).

## Outputs

- List of relevant papers with titles, authors, abstracts.
- Key findings summary for each paper.
- Citation recommendations for implementation.

## Workflow

1. Clarify research objective and scope.
2. Search arXiv using relevant keywords and categories.
3. Filter and rank papers by relevance.
4. Extract key findings and methodologies.
5. Summarize actionable insights for trading applications.
